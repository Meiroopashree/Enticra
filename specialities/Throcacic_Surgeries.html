Pediatric Thoracic Surgery

Pediatric thoracic surgery focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the lungs, chest wall, and respiratory system in children. Our specialized surgical team addresses a range of issues, including congenital anomalies such as lung malformations and chest wall deformities, as well as conditions like cysts, tumors, and infections. We prioritize using minimally invasive techniques whenever possible to minimize recovery time and discomfort for your child. Our comprehensive care spans the entire treatment process, from accurate diagnosis to post-surgical recovery, ensuring a seamless experience for both the child and their family. Our primary goal is to restore optimal respiratory function and enhance the quality of life for children facing thoracic conditions
