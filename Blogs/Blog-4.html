Paediatric Neurosurgery: Specialized Brain and Spinal Care for Children

We provide expert paediatric neurosurgery services, specializing in diagnosing and treating neurological disorders in children. Our highly skilled surgeons offer state-of-the-art care with precision and compassion, addressing conditions affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Understanding Paediatric Neurosurgery

Children's neurological systems are still developing, making paediatric neurosurgery a complex specialty. Our team is trained to meet the unique needs of young patients, ensuring tailored treatment for conditions ranging from congenital malformations to traumatic injuries and tumors.

Common Conditions Treated

Our paediatric neurosurgery services cover a wide range of conditions, including:

  • Congenital Malformations: Early surgical intervention for conditions like spina bifida improves motor function and prevents complications.
  • Hydrocephalus: We treat this condition by inserting shunts to drain excess fluid and relieve pressure on the brain.
  • Brain and Spinal Tumors: Our surgeons precisely remove tumors while preserving surrounding brain tissue.
  • Epilepsy Surgery: For cases unresponsive to medication, we can surgically remove seizure-causing brain areas.
  • Traumatic Injuries: We help repair brain and spine injuries to minimize long-term effects.
  • Craniosynostosis: Our surgical interventions correct skull shape to allow for normal brain growth.

Our Approach to Care

We create a compassionate, child-friendly environment to ease anxiety for both children and their families. Utilizing the latest technology, including advanced imaging like MRIs and CT scans, we ensure accurate diagnoses and employ minimally invasive surgical techniques for quicker recovery and reduced pain.

Advanced Surgical Techniques

Our paediatric neurosurgery employs cutting-edge technology for safe and precise treatment:

  • Endoscopic Neurosurgery: Minimally invasive techniques reduce recovery time and scarring.
  • Shunt Systems: Effectively manage hydrocephalus by draining excess brain fluid.
  • Stereotactic Surgery: 3D imaging is used for precise treatment of small brain areas.

Post-Operative Care and Rehabilitation

Post-surgery, we collaborate with pediatric neurologists and therapists to support your child's recovery. Regular follow-ups ensure progress monitoring and early detection of any issues.
