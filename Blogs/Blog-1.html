Antenatal Counselling: Your Guide to a Healthy Pregnancy

We offer specialized antenatal counselling to support expectant parents through every stage of pregnancy. Our expert guidance ensures that you are well-prepared for the arrival of your baby, both physically and emotionally

Why Choose Antenatal Counselling?

Antenatal counselling is essential for preparing parents for pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum challenges.
Key benefits include:

  • Knowledge About Pregnancy: Understand the stages of pregnancy and monitor your health and your baby's development.
  • Emotional Support: Discuss feelings and concerns in a safe space.
  • Birth Planning: Create a personalized birth plan, covering delivery methods and pain management options.
  • Postpartum Awareness: Learn about physical and emotional changes after childbirth and how to care for your newborn.

What We Cover

Our counselling sessions include:

  • Prenatal Care: Importance of routine check-ups and prenatal vitamins.
  • Nutrition and Lifestyle: Dietary recommendations and safe exercises for a healthy pregnancy.
  • Managing Discomforts: Practical tips for handling common pregnancy symptoms.
  • Emotional Well-Being: Techniques to cope with anxiety and stress.
  • Postpartum Preparation: Guidance on recovery, breastfeeding, and newborn care.
  • Recognizing Complications: Identifying signs of conditions like preeclampsia or gestational diabetes for early intervention.
