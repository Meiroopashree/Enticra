Paediatric Thoracic Surgery
Precision Care for Chest and Lung Conditions in Children

We specialize in paediatric thoracic surgery, addressing chest, lung, and diaphragm conditions in infants and children. From congenital abnormalities to traumatic injuries, our highly skilled paediatric thoracic surgeon performs precise, minimally invasive surgeries to ensure the best outcomes for young patients.

Understanding Paediatric Thoracic Surgery

Children's delicate respiratory systems make them particularly vulnerable to chest and lung conditions that can affect breathing and overall development. We focus on congenital malformations, such as diaphragmatic hernias and lung deformities, and emphasize early detection and surgical intervention to support normal growth.

Conditions Treated

We treat various conditions, including:

  • Congenital Diaphragmatic Hernia (CDH): Surgical repair is needed to reposition abdominal organs in the chest cavity.
  • Pulmonary Malformations: Abnormal lung tissue requiring surgical removal to improve function.
  • Pectus Excavatum: Surgical correction of an inward-sinking chest wall to restore normal function.
  • Lung Infections and Empyema: Surgical drainage may be required for severe infections to promote healing.
  • Tumors: Precise removal of chest or lung tumors to maintain lung function.
  • Tracheoesophageal Fistula: Surgical correction of abnormal connections between the esophagus and trachea.

Minimally Invasive Techniques

We utilize advanced minimally invasive techniques, such as thoracoscopy, which involves small incisions and camera guidance. Benefits include:

  • Reduced post-operative pain
  • Faster recovery
  • Minimal scarring
  • Lower complication risk

Advanced Surgical Techniques

Many conditions are congenital and may be diagnosed during antenatal screenings. Early diagnosis allows for prompt surgical intervention, critical for preventing complications like lung infections and impaired heart function. We work closely with parents to monitor their child's condition, providing early and comprehensive treatment plans for optimal outcomes.

Post-Surgical Care

Post-surgical care is crucial for healing. Our team monitors lung function and overall health, offering detailed instructions for:

  • Pain management
  • Breathing exercises
  • Follow-up appointments
Our goal is to ensure a smooth recovery and get children back to normal activities as quickly as possible.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing a paediatric thoracic surgeon is a vital decision. We offer:

  • Experience and Expertise: Our surgeon has specialized training in complex thoracic conditions.
  • Advanced Technology: We utilize the latest minimally invasive techniques for optimal outcomes.
  • Comprehensive Care: From diagnosis to recovery, we provide complete support for your child.

Conclusion

Paediatric thoracic surgery is essential for treating life-threatening and developmental conditions in children. We are committed to using advanced surgical techniques and providing compassionate care, ensuring every child has the best chance for a healthy, active life. Our dedicated team is here to support your family from diagnosis through recovery.
